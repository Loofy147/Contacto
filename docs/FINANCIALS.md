### **Budget Optimization Options**

-   **Option A: Full Team (Recommended)**
    -   **Total:** 6,588,000 DZD
    -   **Best for:** Speed to market, high quality.

-   **Option B: Lean Startup (Reduced)**
    -   **Total:** ~3,500,000 DZD (~$26,000)
    -   **Strategy:** Reduce team to core members (CTO, 2 devs, 1 designer), outsource non-core tasks, reduce marketing, and use co-working spaces.
    -   **Best for:** Limited capital, slower growth.

-   **Option C: Hybrid (Balanced)**
    -   **Total:** ~4,500,000 DZD (~$33,000)
    -   **Strategy:** Mix of full-time and freelance talent, moderate marketing spend.
    -   **Best for:** Balanced risk/reward.
