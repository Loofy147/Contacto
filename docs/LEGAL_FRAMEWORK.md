## **Phase 2 - Legal & Compliance Requirements**

### **1. ุงูููุชุฑุฉ ุงูุฅููุชุฑูููุฉ (E-Invoicing)**
-   **Law**: Finance Law 2021 (Article 191).
-   **Requirements**:
    -   Sequentially numbered invoices (e.g., `FV-2026-00001`).
    -   Mandatory data: Invoice number, date, seller/buyer info, product details, prices, TVA, and total.
    -   **Electronic archiving for 10 years is mandatory**.
-   **Contacto Implementation**:
    -   Provide legally compliant invoice templates.
    -   Ensure secure, sequential numbering.
    -   Encrypt and back up all financial data daily.

### **2. Automated Tax Reporting**
-   **TVA (VAT)**:
    -   Automated calculation of TVA collected and TVA paid.
    -   Generate reports (G50 format) for monthly or quarterly declarations.
-   **TAP (Professional Activity Tax)**:
    -   Automated calculation based on revenue.
-   **IRG/IBS (Income/Corporate Tax)**:
    -   Contacto will provide supporting reports, but final calculation requires an accountant.

### **3. Commercial Data Protection**
-   **Customer Data**: Full compliance with Law 18-07, including encryption at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3).
-   **Business Sensitive Data**: All sensitive business data (cost prices, profit margins, supplier info) must be encrypted.
-   **API Security**: API keys must be encrypted, and strict rate limiting will be enforced.
-   **Backups**: All backups must be encrypted and stored in a secure, off-site location.

### **4. Service Agreements**
-   **API Terms of Service**:
    -   Defines allowed and prohibited use (no reselling, reverse engineering, etc.).
    -   Specifies rate limits and quotas for different subscription tiers.
    -   Clarifies data ownership (customer owns their data).
-   **Service Level Agreement (SLA)**:
    -   Guarantees uptime (e.g., 99.5% for Growth tier).
    -   Defines support response times.
    -   Provides a credit system for SLA breaches.
-   **Data Processing Agreement (DPA)**:
    -   Updated DPA for API users, clarifying roles and responsibilities.

### **5. Intellectual Property**
-   The Contacto API, documentation, and source code are the intellectual property of Contacto.

## **Phase 3 - Legal & Compliance Requirements**

### **1. Financial Licensing**
-   **Objective**: Obtain a financial license from the Bank of Algeria or partner with a licensed entity.
-   **Requirements**:
    -   Significant capital (100M+ DZD for a full license).
    -   A robust business plan, financial projections, and technical specifications.
    -   Passing a rigorous due diligence process, including technical and financial audits.

### **2. Anti-Money Laundering (AML) & Counter-Terrorist Financing (CTF)**
-   **Law**: Full compliance with Law 05-01.
-   **Requirements**:
    -   Implement a robust transaction monitoring system to detect suspicious activity.
    -   Establish a process for filing Suspicious Activity Reports (SARs) with the CTRF.
    -   Conduct Customer Due Diligence (CDD) and Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD) for high-risk customers.
    -   Screen all users against national and international sanctions lists.
    -   Maintain all records for a minimum of 10 years.

### **3. PCI-DSS Compliance**
-   **Level**: Level 1 compliance is mandatory for processing card payments.
-   **Requirements**:
    -   Implement tokenization to avoid storing raw card numbers.
    -   Maintain a secure network with firewalls and intrusion detection systems.
    -   Encrypt all cardholder data in transit and at rest.
    -   Implement strict access control measures.
    -   Regularly test security systems and processes (e.g., penetration testing).
